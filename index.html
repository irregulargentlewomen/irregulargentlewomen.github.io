Irregular Gentlewomen

Feminism. Curiosity. Open Source.

Elizabeth Yalkut and Betsy Haibel are the Irregular Gentlewomen: two creators who ask questions and solve problems. We are interested in red pandas, typography, the history of Cornwall, supporting women in STEM and "open stuff," single-estate Assam tea, the implications of Catullus XVI, herb-based cocktails, space exploration, kicking impostor syndrome in the teeth, Paul Signac's paintings, data visualization, tae kwon do, writing better unit tests, and queer science fiction. Among other topics.
